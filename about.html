Our Mission

Making the right choice should never be hard

"We are India's first platform that brings together all reviews — from the biggest sources in the world and from real people like you — so you never have to search five different apps again."

Every day, millions of Indians open Google, IMDb, Amazon, Zomato, and five other apps just to make one decision. Is this movie worth watching? Is this shop trustworthy? Should I buy this product?

That information exists everywhere — but it is scattered. CritiQ consolidates it all in one clean, simple platform. And on top of that, we let real people — people like you — add their own genuine opinions.

Because at the end of the day, the most trusted review is the one from someone who has actually been there.

Our story

CritiQ started with a simple frustration. Every time someone wanted to watch a movie, they would open IMDb, then Google, then ask friends on WhatsApp — and still feel unsure. Every time someone wanted to visit a new shop, they would search on Google Maps, then look for more opinions elsewhere.

The information was out there. It was just scattered.

We asked ourselves — what if one platform could bring it all together? What if you could see the IMDb score, the Google rating, and what real people in your city are saying — all on one screen, in your own language?

That is why CritiQ was born. Not just as a review app — but as a decision-making companion for every Indian. Whether you are in Mumbai or Madurai, Delhi or Dhanbad — CritiQ is here to help you choose confidently.

We are just getting started. Movies are live today. Restaurants, shops, products, and much more are coming soon. And every step of the way, your genuine reviews will be at the heart of it all.
