Frequently asked questions

Is CritiQ free to use? ▼
Yes! CritiQ is completely free for all users. You can search, read reviews, and write your own reviews without paying anything. Ever.
How does the Unified Score work? ▼
We pull ratings from IMDb, Google, and our own community reviews and calculate one clean average score. So instead of checking three apps, you see one honest number.
Can I write a review in my language? ▼
Absolutely! CritiQ supports Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi and more. Write your review in whichever language feels most natural to you.
When will shops, restaurants and products be available? ▼
We are working on it! Movies are live right now. Restaurants, shops, malls, and products are coming soon. Sign up to be the first to know when they launch.
How do you handle fake reviews? ▼
Genuine reviews are the heart of CritiQ. We have a reporting system where users can flag suspicious reviews. Every flagged review is checked by our team. We take this very seriously.
